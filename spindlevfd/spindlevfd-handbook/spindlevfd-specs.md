## 1.5KW Spindle Kit

Each kit comes with:

- 80mm Clear Cut Dust Shoe
- ER20, 80mm, 1.5KW 110V air-cooled spindle
- 110V, 1.5KW, 3-Phase output enclosed VFD with EMI filtering for electrical noise reduction
- AC power cable and 5m spindle power cable
- ER20 1/8, 1/4″, 3/8″ and 1/2″ collets
- Data cable to connect to the SuperLongBoard controller for remote speed control
- 80mm spindle mount (only for LongMill Spindle Kit)

***Spindle and VFD Specifications***

- Spindle body diameter: 80mm
- Collet nut: ER20-A, M25x1.50mm
- Spindle weight: 5.4KG or 11.9lbs
- RPM range: 7,500 to 24,000 RPM
- Power: 1.5KW (2HP)
- Voltage: 110V/120V AC, single phase, NEMA 5-15P plug included
![](/_images/_spindlevfd/_spindlevfd_handbook/spindlevfd_nema5-15.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}
- Power requirements: One 15A 110V/120V single phase circuit (you can also put dust collection on this circuit)

## 2.2KW Spindle Kit

Each kit comes with:

- 80mm Clear Cut Dust Shoe
- ER20, 80mm, 2.2KW 220V air-cooled spindle
220V input
- 2.2KW, 3-Phase output enclosed VFD with EMI filtering for electrical noise reduction
- NEMA 6-15P AC power cable
![](/_images/_spindlevfd/_spindlevfd_handbook/spindlevfd_nema6-15.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}
- Spindle power cable (5m for AltMill 2×4/4×4, 7.2m for 4×8)
- ER20 1/8, 1/4″, 3/8″ and 1/2″ collets

***Spindle and VFD Specifications***

- Spindle body diameter: 80mm
- Collet nut: ER20-A, M25x1.50mm
- Spindle weight: 5.4KG or 11.9lbs
- RPM range: 7,500 to 24,000 RPM
- Power: 2.2KW (3HP)
- Voltage: 220V/240V AC, single phase, NEMA 6-15P plug included
- Power requirements: One 15A 220V/240V single phase circuit dedicated to the spindle

## Clear Cut Dust Shoe

- Spindle diameter: Compatible with 80mm spindles
- Hose diameter: Compatible with 4″ standard hose
