Below are the video instructions for assembling your dust shoe.

## Clear Cut Dust Shoe

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EDFtzKVT4R4

For the ATC version of the Clear Cut Dust Shoe, please see [this page.](https://resources.sienci.com/view/atc-dust-shoe/)

Usage guidelines:

- You can remove the bottom section of the dust shoe when changing out your tool
- If you are using the Clear Cut Dust Shoe on the LongMill, please note that machine travel may be reduced

## 80mm Dust Shoe

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TNJ8yon4fBw

Usage guidelines:

- We recommend to take off the dust shoe when surfacing, since the machine is moving to its limits the dust shoe can get damaged
- Feel free to adjust the dust shoe using the hose clamp. We recommend positioning it so that it faces along the Y-axis so it does not interfere with your cutting jobs
