When operating the 7W LaserBeam you will create smoke and other particulates that will get into the lens, lens focus ring, aluminum heat sink and laser assembly cooling fan which can affect performance. We recommend that you clean the lens with rubbing alcohol and a non-abrasive cloth such as a lens cleaning cloth, cotton swab or microfiber cloth.
