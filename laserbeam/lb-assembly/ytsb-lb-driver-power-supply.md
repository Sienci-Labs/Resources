<strong>Parts list:</strong>
<ul>
  <li>LaserBeam 12V 8A power supply</li>
</ul>
Locate the power supply and insert the female AC cable plug into the power supply.

<img class="aligncenter wp-image-2266 size-medium" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Insert-AC-Cable-12V-8A-Power-Supply-e1726682475855-850x477.jpg" alt="" width="850" height="477" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><i>AC cable plugged into power supply</i></p>
Plug in the other end of the AC cable to a power outlet that supplies 100-240VAC.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2260" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Ac-Cable-Power-Outlet-e1726682529953.jpg" alt="" width="960" height="540" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><i>Power supply plugged into 100-240VAC outlet</i></p>
Plug the green male connector from the power supply into the driver power input, making sure the connection location and polarity are correct, as shown.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10703" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/power-to-driver.png" alt="" width="960" height="540" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><i>Connecting power to driver</i></p>
On the power supply, confirm that the LED is lit up, which indicates that your power supply is receiving power. Depending on your version of the power supply it may glow green or blue.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10819 aligncenter" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/LED-power-supply.png" alt="" width="960" height="540" />
