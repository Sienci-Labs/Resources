<h3>Foam Case</h3>
The most delicate and important parts of the LaserBeam are included in the foam case:
<ul>
  <li>Laser Diode Assembly with Air Assist &amp; Shield Attachment</li>
  <li>5A Constant Current Laser Driver</li>
</ul>

This padded envelope includes your 6ft AC cable
<ul>
  <li>6ft AC cable</li>
</ul>

This box contains your Laser Driver power supply
<ul>
  <li>12V 8A Power Supply</li>
</ul>

This bag includes small parts needed to fully set up your LaserBeam
<ul>

  <li>2pcs M5-12mm Socket Head Screws - Router Mount Hardware</li>
  <li>2pcs 7mm Lens Spring - For G2 Lens</li>
  <li>2pcs 10mm Lens Spring - For 3E, G7 or G8 Lens</li>
  <li>2pcs 13mm Lens Spring - For 3E, G7 or G8 Lens</li>
</ul>
