<strong>Parts List: </strong>
<ul>
  <li>Laser assembly with steel mount</li>
  <li>2pcs M5-12mm socket head screws</li>
</ul>
We will first attach the laser assembly onto the router mount.

Depending on your version of the router mount, you will be able to fasten the assembly on either the left/right side or the front of your router mount.

First grab the M5-12mm screws from the Small Parts bag, and identify where the two mounting holes are on your setup.

<img class="size-full wp-image-10836 aligncenter" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/router-mount.png" alt="" width="960" height="540" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><i>Mounting holes on the side (L) or on the front (R) of the LongMill router mount</i></p>
<img class="size-full wp-image-10938 aligncenter" src="https://resources.sienci.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/mounting-holes-laser-altmill.png" alt="" width="559" height="540" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><em>Mounting holes on the AltMill router mount</em></p>
Align the 2 holes at the steel laser mount (on the laser assembly) to the 2 holes on the router mount. Then thread the two M5-12mms screw by hand.

Use a M5 Metric Allen Key to tighten both screws until firmly secured.
