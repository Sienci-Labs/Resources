## Clear Cut Dust Shoe Installation

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust_header.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-parts1.jpg){.aligncenter .size-full .wid}

### Assembly

1. Attach the **front and rear gaskets** to the Clear Cut ATC bracket:

   * Align holes between the front gasket and bracket
   * Both gaskets should sit flush to the bottom of the bracket

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-gasket.jpeg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

1. Insert **four (4) M5 - 16mm socket head screws**, one in each corner. Then fasten the upper dust shoe and lower dust shoe to the bracket using an Allen key.

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-upperlower.jpeg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

1. Slide the dust shoe assembly on, from the bottom of the spindle.

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-mount.jpeg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

### Height Adjustment

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-parts2.jpg){.aligncenter .size-full .wid}

1. Insert one (1) **M6 - 14 mm screw** into each slider bushing, for a total of two (2) screws.

1. Place slider bushings on the **left and right sides** of the bracket.

   * Secure into the **bottom threaded holes** on the spindle.

1. Place a washer on each handle, then thread the handle into the **top hole** of each slider.

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-fasten.jpeg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

1. Adjust dust shoe height to your desired position, then tighten the handle to lock it in place.

### Hose Installation

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dust-parts3.jpg){.aligncenter .size-full .wid}

1. Check that the VFD is turned off.  

1. Assemble the **hose bracket** onto the top of the spindle using three (3) M4 - 4mm button head screws.

   * ⚠️ Do not drop screws into the spindle.
   * You may want to put a piece of paper to cover the spindle holes while assembling the fasteners. Remove the paper when you are done!

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dustshoe-7.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

1. Slide a hose clamp onto the hose.

1. Attach the hose to the dust shoe inlet and tighten the clamp.

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dustshoe-8.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

1. Zip-tie the hose to the hose bracket for strain relief.

![](/_images/_atc/_atc_assembly/_dust/atc_assembly_dustshoe-9.jpg){.aligncenter .size-medium}

### Usage Notes

* The **lower dust shoe** can be removed for probing.
* For tool changes, the dust shoe may remain installed.
