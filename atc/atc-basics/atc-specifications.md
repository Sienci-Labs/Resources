## What's Included

In each ATC Spindle Kit, you will receive:

- 2.2KW ATC Spindle  
- ATC Spindle Mount  
- Enclosed 2.2KW VFD and Mount Adapter  
- Clear Cut Dust Shoe  
- Tool Length Sensor  
- ATC Expansion Board for an SLB-EXT Controller  
- 2.2KW VFD NEMA 6-15P Power Cord  
- 10M Length 8mm Air Line Tubing  
- Male 1/4" NPT to 8mm Air Line Push Fitting  
- ER20 Collet Wrench  
- All cables and harnesses  

For the specific ATC Spindle Kits:

**ATC 6-Tool Rack Kit**

- 1 Tool Rack (holds 6 tools total)  
- 6 ISO20 Tool Holders  
- 12 ER20 Collets, for 1/8", 1/4", 3/8", 1/2" bits  

**ATC 12-Tool Rack Kit**

- 2 Tool Racks (holds 12 tools total)  
- 12 ISO20 Tool Holders  
- 24 ER20 Collets, for 1/8", 1/4", 3/8", 1/2" bits  

**ATC Spindle Only**

- 3 ISO20 Tool Holders (No tool rack)  
- 12 ER20 Collets, for 1/8", 1/4", 3/8", 1/2" bits  

Not included in the ATC Spindle Kit:

- [AltMill CNC Machine](https://sienci.com/product-category/altmill/)  
- [Computer or laptop](https://sienci.com/product/gcontrol/)  
- [End mills or bits](https://sienci.com/product-category/end-mills-bits/)  
- [Air regulator and filter](https://sienci.com/product/atc-air-filter-regulator-kit/)  
- Air compressor  
- LED light strips

## Compatibility

**Machines:** All sizes and models of the AltMill are compatible with our ATC. Our ATC is not compatible with third-party CNC machines.

**Reduction in Machine Travel (Y-axis):**  
5.3" from the back of the machine, accounting for clearance for the Clear Cut Dust Shoe.

**Remaining Machine Travel (Y-axis):**

- **2×4:** 20.3"  
- **4×4:** 43.5"  
- **4×8:** 99"  

Note that the tool rack is detachable, so it can be removed to free up the full cutting area.

## Minimum Requirements

### Electrical

**ATC Power:** Air manifold, pressure and temperature sensors utilize the SLB-EXT's existing power supply.

**Total System Power:**  
3 separate electrical circuits, to provide:

- 110 VAC 15A single phase for the AltMill (SLB-EXT with ATC hookup), dust collection, and computer  
- 220 VAC 15A single phase for the 2.2KW spindle  
- 110 **or** 220 VAC 15A single phase for your air compressor  

### Customer Supplied Air Compressor

- **Operating Pressure:** 100 psi  
- **Airflow:** 3 CFM (ft³/min) at 90 psi or better  
- **Filter:** 40 µm or better, coalescing type  
- **Dryer:** More information to be provided

## Product Details

### Spindle

- **Output Power:** 2.2 kW  
- **Speed:** 7,500–24,000 RPM, constant torque beginning at 18,000 RPM  
- **Collet:** ER20  
- **Bearings:** Ceramic  
- **VFD power plug style:** NEMA 6-15P  

### ATC

- **Cooling:** Air cooled with 24V fan  
- **Operating Noise:** 60 dBA at 24,000 RPM  
- **Tool Holder:** ISO20  
- **Collet Type:** ER20 collets  
- **Tool Rack Size:** Maximum tool diameter 2", maximum shank diameter 1/2"  
- **Number of Slots:** 6 or 12 (configuration dependent)
